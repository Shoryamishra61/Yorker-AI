🔬 Methodology

📐
Walk-Forward Validation
Train only on past data, predict next season. Repeated across 11 IPL seasons (2016–2026). No future leakage.
⚖️
Leakage-Safe Features
Pre-match features only. Confirmed XIs separated into a different feature set. No post-toss or in-match data in predictions.
🎯
Calibration Checks
Brier score, ECE, and reliability curves ensure "60% probability" actually means winning ~60% of the time.
📊
Baseline Comparison
Model compared against Elo, recent form, venue win rates, and season win rate baselines. Honest lift measurement.
